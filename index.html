Project Description

SmartDress AI is an intelligent wardrobe assistant designed to eliminate the daily “what-to-wear” dilemma. Leveraging real-time weather data, your calendar, and context cues you provide, the system generates curated outfit recommendations that balance comfort, style, and occasion-appropriate norms. A built-in wardrobe optimizer learns from your existing clothes inventory, surfaces under-worn items to extend their lifecycle, and flags gaps worth filling. If the forecast shifts suddenly—rain clouds rolling in or a sharp temperature drop—SmartDress AI triggers Quick Swap suggestions so you can adjust a single layer instead of re-planning everything. Minutes before you head out, a Pre-Departure Final-Check reviews live conditions and traffic delays, then gives a last thumbs-up (or alternate pick) to ensure you stay punctual and polished. By reducing decision fatigue and late-morning scrambling, SmartDress AI turns dressing from a stressor into a seamless, data-driven routine.
